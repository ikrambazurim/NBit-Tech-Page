About NBit Technology

Incorporated in March 2003, NBit Technology is a leading provider of end-to-end IT services and maintenance solutions, delivering innovation and reliability to meet the evolving needs of industries. With a strong presence in Malaysia’s technology hub, we are committed to enabling digital transformation through advanced and future-ready IT capabilities.

Headquartered at Lot 70447, Kulim Hi-Tech Park, Jalan Hi-Tech 6, Kawasan Industri, Kulim Techno City Fasa 2, 09090 Kulim, Kedah, we operate at the center of industrial excellence, supporting a diverse portfolio of clients across multiple sectors.
